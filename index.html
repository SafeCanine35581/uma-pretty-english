About Umamusume: Pretty English

What is Umamusume: Pretty English?

Umamusume: Pretty English is a fan-made English dub of Umamusume: Pretty Derby, created by Cygames. We voice over skits, game content and so much more and post content on our YouTube channel! You can also join our Discord server to chat with us and other Umamusume fans.

Watch us on YouTube!
Join us on Discord!

How to Join?

Do you want to voice your favorite Uma? Do you want to help out with editing, translating, designing, or anything else? Join the Discord server and ask or audition for a role!
